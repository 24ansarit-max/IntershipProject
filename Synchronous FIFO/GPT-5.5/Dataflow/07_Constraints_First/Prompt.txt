[DATAFLOW]   Every combinational output MUST be a continuous assign. 
             No always_comb. No always @(*). No exceptions. 
             Allowed always blocks: only @(posedge clk) for FFs/BRAM. 
 
[TIMING]     Fmax ≥ 100 MHz on xc7a100tcsg324-2. 
             Maximum combinational depth of any assign chain: 3 LUT levels. 
             full/empty must be registered at output (add 1 FF stage). 
             count subtractor is the longest path — verify ≤ 3 levels. 
 
[AREA]       ≤ 40 LUTs total for all assign logic. 
             ≤ 30 FFs (pointer FFs + output register FFs). 
             BRAM storage: (* ram_style = "block" *) mandatory. 
 
[POWER]      assign wr_en_safe = wr_en & ~full   MANDATORY . 
             assign rd_en_safe = rd_en & ~empty   MANDATORY . 
             These gating assigns are the primary power constraint. 
             Without them BRAM sees spurious enables = wasted power. 
 
[CODING]     5-bit binary pointers: MSB=wrap, [3:0]=BRAM address. 
             Synchronous active-low rst_n on all pointer and flag FFs. 
             Non-blocking <= in all always @(posedge clk) blocks. 
             No inferred latches (assign is inherently latch-free).

Write a 16-bit Carry Lookahead Adder in BEHAVIORAL Verilog. 
 
DO NOT do any of the following: 
- Do NOT use a simple + operator (that generates a ripple adder) 
- Do NOT create latches (use always @(*) not always @(posedge clk) for combinational logic) 
- Do NOT use for loops to ripple carries (that defeats CLA purpose) 
- Do NOT skip the group-level lookahead (must compute C4, C8, C12 in parallel) 
- Do NOT leave carry bits as sequential dependencies 
- Do NOT use blocking assignments in clocked blocks 
- Do NOT hardcode — use parameter WIDTH=16 
 
MUST include: 
- Explicit G[i], P[i] reg arrays 
- Explicit CLA equations for all 4 groups 
- Group GG[3:0], GP[3:0] for second-level lookahead 
- // Power, // Area, // Delay comments 
- Signed overflow flag

Generate a 16-bit CLA in STRUCTURAL Verilog. 
 
AVOID THESE MISTAKES: 
- Do NOT use assign in the top module (defeats structural style) 
- Do NOT use always blocks in structural modules 
- Do NOT instantiate full adders in a chain (that's ripple carry) 
- Do NOT use arithmetic operators (+, -) 
- Do NOT create modules with no clear single responsibility 
 
CORRECT APPROACH: 
- pg_cell module: only AND and XOR primitives or assign for G and P 
- carry_logic module: only AND/OR tree for carry bits 
- cla_4bit_block: instantiates pg_cell x4 + carry_logic 
- cla_16bit: instantiates 4x cla_4bit_block + group carry logic 
 
Each module must have a // Responsibility: comment, // Area: comment, // Delay: comment.

Write a Verilog DATAFLOW model of a Carry Lookahead Adder (CLA). 
 
Requirements: 
- Use ONLY continuous assign statements (no always blocks, no gate primitives) 
- For a 4-bit CLA, compute: 
    * Propagate signals: P[i] = A[i] ^ B[i] 
    * Generate signals:  G[i] = A[i] & B[i] 
    * Carry lookahead equations explicitly: 
        C1 = G0 | (P0 & Cin) 
        C2 = G1 | (P1 & G0) | (P1 & P0 & Cin) 
        C3 = G2 | (P2 & G1) | (P2 & P1 & G0) | (P2 & P1 & P0 & Cin) 
        C4 = G3 | (P3 & G2) | ... (full expansion) 
    * Sum bits: S[i] = P[i] ^ C[i] 
- Scale to 16-bit using four 4-bit CLA dataflow blocks connected via assign 
- Every intermediate signal must be a named wire 
- Include a testbench that checks all-zeros, all-ones, alternating bits,  
  max value, and overflow cases using $display

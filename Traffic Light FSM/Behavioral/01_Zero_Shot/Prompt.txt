targeting the Nexys A7 FPGA (xc7a100tcsg324-2).

- Behavioral style using always @(posedge clk) 
- 16-bit state encoding (parameter-based, one-hot or binary)

- Outputs: red[N:0], yellow[N:0], green[N:0] per direction 
- Timer-based transitions using a 16-bit countdown register 
- Include inline comments on every state transition 
 
PPA target: minimize flip-flop count (≤ 17 FF), Fmax > 100 MHz, 
LUT utilization < 40 LUTs on xc7a100tcsg324-2.
